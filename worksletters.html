  • Catégories dérivées en cohomologie l-adique, J.-P. Jouanolou


    Transcription

    This project, involving the transcription of J.-P. Jouanolou’s thesis, was conducted with his authorization. It was typeset in LaTeX under the direction of Mateo Carmona, with the collaboration of Niels Borne, along with volunteers. The transcription aims to be as faithful as possible to the original. Its edition is considered provisional, and we welcome any remarks, comments, and corrections.

  • Pursuing stacks, A. Grothendieck


    Transcription

    The project of transcribing Grothendieck's "Pursuing Stacks" was originally undertaken in 2015 by "thescrivener," this edition is an extended and revised version of it by Mateo Carmona, with the collaboration of Ulrik Buchholtz. The transcription aims to be as faithful as possible to the original. Its edition is considered provisional, and we welcome any remarks, comments, and corrections.

  • CSG Transcriptions


    The CSG actively supports transcription projects aimed at enhancing accessibility to hard-to-read materials and unpublished works within the Grothendieckian community. This includes handwritten texts and selected existing editions that are being improved for better legibility. These collaborative endeavors are focused on enriching CSG's archive, thereby facilitating the exploration and research of Grothendieck’s works by scholars and enthusiasts alike.
