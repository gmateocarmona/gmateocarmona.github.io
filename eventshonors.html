  • Visions en mathématiques : de Grothendieck à nos jours


    Colloque grand public de l’Institut Grothendieck à Paris. Juin, 2023

    Le Colloque « Visions en mathématiques : de Grothendieck à nos jours » s’est tenu le mercredi 14 juin 2023 à l’Amphithéâtre Hermite de l’Institut Henri Poincaré, 11 Rue Pierre et Marie Curie, 75005 Paris, de 18h à 20h, avec les interventions d’Olivia Caramello, Mateo Carmona, Alain Connes, Stéphane Dugowson, Johanna Grothendieck et Laurent Lafforgue.

  • Homage to Alexander Grothendieck


    To celebrate the 10th anniversary of the death of the great mathematician Alexander Grothendieck, occurred on 13 November 2014, the Grothendieck Institute organises a commemorative conference in his honour.
    The event will be held on Saturday the 16th of November 2024, and will feature as speakers Prof. Fernando Zalamea, Mateo Carmona, and Prof. Olivia Caramello, who will receive the testimonies of four scholars who have known Grothendieck personally, and who will share their memories and evoke his intellectual heritage: Christian Escriva, Dominique Lepetz, Jean Malgoire and Marc Tettiravou.
